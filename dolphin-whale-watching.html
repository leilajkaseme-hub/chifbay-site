01The experience

28 species. One private boat.

Madeira's waters host over 28 cetacean species. Bottlenose dolphins, common dolphins and pilot whales live here year-round, with more species appearing from spring through autumn.

On a private boat your captain isn't tied to any timetable - he can slow down, hold position, and let you watch without engine noise or crowds.

02The wildlife

Quiet enough to actually see them

Big tour boats push wildlife away with engine noise and crowds. A small private boat can hold position silently, so the dolphins stay close and you get the moment - and the photos.

Sightings can never be guaranteed with wild animals, but our skipper's knowledge of the coast gives you the best possible chance, year-round.

Good to know

Questions, answered

Are you guaranteed to see dolphins or whales?+

Sightings are very common - Madeira hosts over 28 cetacean species year-round - but as these are wild animals nothing is guaranteed. Our local skipper knows where they feed and travel, which gives the best possible chance.

How long is the tour?+

The private tour lasts about 3 hours and departs from Marina do Funchal, usually first thing in the morning when cetaceans are most active.

Is the boat private?+

Yes - the boat is yours alone, up to 7 guests plus the skipper. No shared groups, ever.
