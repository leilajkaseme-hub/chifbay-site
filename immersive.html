I — The idea

There are boat tours, and|there are Chifbay days.

i.

The coast

Madeira rises from the Atlantic like a green amphitheatre. From the water you see the half of it no road reaches: caves cut into black basalt, coves with no path down, and Cabo Girão falling 580 metres straight into the sea.

ii.

The skipper

Not a guide with a script — a licensed local who reads the sea each morning and changes the route accordingly. If the dolphins are west, you go west. If the swell is up, he knows the sheltered cove that still looks like a postcard.

iii.

The day

Seven guests at most, and the boat is yours alone. No queues, no strangers, no timetable. Madeira wine and poncha on board, music if you want it, silence if you don't. You decide when to swim and when to sail on.

Cliffs no road will ever show you.
