Good to know

Questions, answered

Is snorkeling gear included?+

Yes - masks, fins and a paddleboard on request are aboard for everyone, and your skipper anchors in the clearest, calmest coves for the conditions.

Can you really not reach these by road?+

Many of Madeira's best coves have no road access at all - they can only be reached from the water, which is exactly why they stay so quiet.

What if the sea is rough?+

For your safety and comfort the trip is weather-dependent and the skipper picks the most sheltered spots, or reschedules if conditions require it.
