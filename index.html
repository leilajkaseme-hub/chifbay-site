01The starting point

Funchal, where Madeira
meets the open Atlantic

Madeira rises from the ocean like a green amphitheatre, and its capital Funchal sits right at the water's edge — the natural place to begin any exploration by sea.

A private trip with Chifbay casts off from the marina aboard our 2026 Karnic R8S. No queues, no strangers, no fixed script — just your group, a skipper who knows every cove, and a route built entirely around you.

★The landmark

Cabo Girão,
seen from the water

From the sea the scale is a different thing entirely — a sheer wall of volcanic basalt rising 580 metres straight from the Atlantic, one of the highest sea cliffs in Europe. The route also passes Câmara de Lobos, the fishing village that drew Winston Churchill to paint.
