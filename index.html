A private boat trip with Chifbay starts from the marina aboard our 2026 Karnic R8S. No queues, no strangers, no fixed script. Just your group, a local skipper who knows every cove, and a route built entirely around what you want.

★The landmark

Cabo Girão, seen from the water

From the water the scale is completely different — a sheer wall of volcanic basalt rising 580 metres straight from the Atlantic, one of the highest sea cliffs in Europe. The route also passes Câmara de Lobos, the fishing village that inspired Winston Churchill to paint.
