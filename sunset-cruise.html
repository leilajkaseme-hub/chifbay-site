Golden hour, poured

The champagne is iced, the Madeira cheese is sliced, the music is whatever you bring. Two hours, no rush, no one else on board.

02Last light

The best table in
Madeira has no floor

Madeira sunsets are dramatic because of the clouds — the colour comes from light hitting them. Slip out of the marina, watch the city turn amber, and let the engine drop to a hush.

Madeira's winter is mild — sea temperature rarely drops below 18°C and winter sunsets are often the most dramatic. The boat carries blankets; bring a light jacket.

04Good to know

Questions, answered

What time does it depart?+

One hour before official sunset, which changes year-round. Summer (Jun–Aug) is around 20:00, winter (Dec–Feb) around 17:30. We send the exact departure time 24 hours ahead.

What if it's cloudy and there's no sunset?+

Madeira sunsets are dramatic because of the clouds. Total overcast is rare — if it happens, we reschedule or refund. We watch the forecast obsessively.

What's included on the sunset cruise?+

Chilled Moët champagne and Madeira wine, the private boat for up to 7 guests, and a local skipper, across about 2 hours of golden-hour coastline.
