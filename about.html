01Yours alone

A private boat, a local skipper, the whole coast

Chifbay started with a frustration: every way to see Madeira by sea meant sharing a deck with thirty strangers on a fixed loop. So we built the opposite — one private boat, your group only, with a route shaped around what you actually want to see.

No shared tours, no fixed groups. The boat, the route and the day are private — designed around the people on board.

02Born here

A decade reading these waters

Our skipper was born and raised in Madeira and has spent a decade reading this coast — where the dolphins feed, which coves stay calm in a north wind, where the light lands best at golden hour.

The coves on the map are nothing next to the ones that aren't. That local knowledge is the difference between a boat ride and a day you remember in winter.
