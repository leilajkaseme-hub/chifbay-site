Privacy Policy

Chifbay respects your privacy. This policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, and the choices you have. Last updated: June 2026.

Who we are

Chifbay operates private boat tours from Marina do Funchal, Madeira, Portugal. For any privacy question you can reach us at hello@chifbay.com.

What information we collect

We only collect the information you choose to give us, including:

  • Your name, email address and phone number when you contact us;
  • The details of your enquiry or booking (dates, group size, preferences);
  • Messages you send us by email, WhatsApp or our contact form.

Bookings and any payments are handled by our booking provider on book.chifbay.com. Card and payment details are processed by that provider — Chifbay never sees or stores your full payment information.

How we use your information

  • To answer your enquiries and arrange your private trip;
  • To manage your booking and keep you informed about it;
  • To improve our service and respond to feedback.

We will never sell your personal data, and we only use it for the purposes above.

Legal basis (GDPR)

Where the EU General Data Protection Regulation applies, we process your data on the basis of your consent and to take steps to enter into or perform a contract (your booking) at your request.

Cookies & analytics

This website may use a small number of cookies and basic analytics to understand how visitors use the site and to remember your language preference. Our booking subdomain (book.chifbay.com) and third-party tools may set their own cookies, governed by their own policies.

Third parties we rely on

  • Booking & payments — our booking platform on book.chifbay.com;
  • Messaging — WhatsApp (Meta) when you contact us there;
  • Hosting — this website is hosted on a static hosting service.

These providers only receive the data needed to deliver their service.

How long we keep your data

We keep your information only for as long as needed to handle your enquiry or booking and to meet our legal obligations, after which it is deleted.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct or delete your personal data, to object to its processing, and to withdraw consent at any time. To exercise any of these rights, email hello@chifbay.com.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new revision date.

Contact

Questions about your privacy? Write to hello@chifbay.com or call +351 937 200 320.
