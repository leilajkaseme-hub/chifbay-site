Good to know

Before you book

How do I book a trip?+

Book online through our availability calendar, or message us on WhatsApp / email with your dates and group size and we'll arrange everything directly.

Where do trips depart from?+

Every Chifbay trip departs from Marina do Funchal on Madeira's south coast. Arrive about 15 minutes before departure to settle in and meet your skipper.

How many guests can join?+

The boat takes up to 7 guests and is reserved exclusively for your group. The price is a flat rate for the boat — great value for couples, families and small groups.

What's your weather policy?+

For your safety and comfort trips are weather-dependent and may be rescheduled. We monitor sea conditions and contact you in advance if anything needs to change.
